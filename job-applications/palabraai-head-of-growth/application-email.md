### Experiment #2: Onboarding Activation Loop (Bioprom-Impex, 2014-2017)

**Hypothesis:**
Most new customers churned within 90 days because they didn't understand our product catalog. A **guided onboarding + early win guarantee** would increase activation and retention.

**Execution:**
1. **Mapped 30-day customer journey** (signup → first order → repeat order)
2. **Identified drop-off points** (43% churned before second order)
3. **Built "Quick Win" onboarding:**
   - Dedicated onboarding specialist (1 call + email sequence)
   - Free product sample kit (€45 value, created urgency)
   - 30-day "love it or return it" guarantee (removed risk)
4. **Automated touchpoints:**
   - Day 7: Check-in email + catalog tips
   - Day 14: Usage analytics + personalized recommendations
   - Day 30: Exclusive discount on next order

**Timeframe:** 90 days to design, 12 months to fully roll out

**Outcome:**
- **Activation rate:** Increased from 34% to **67%** (2x improvement)
- **90-day retention:** Increased from 41% to **73%**
- **Time to first purchase:** Dropped from 18 days to **9 days**
- **LTV increased by 2.3x** (retention drove lifetime value)
- **NPS score:** +47 (industry avg: +22)

**Key Insight:**
**Activation drives everything.** Get users to their first "aha moment" fast — and retention follows naturally.
