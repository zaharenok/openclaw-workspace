# German Tutor Bot 🇦🇹🇩🇪

AI-powered German language learning bot for Telegram, focused on Russian-speaking immigrants in Austria.

## 🎯 Quick Overview

**Problem:** Learning German is expensive (€30-60/hour for tutors) and inconvenient  
**Solution:** 24/7 AI tutor for €9.99-19.99/month - 10x cheaper, always available  
**Target:** 300,000 Russian speakers in Austria (Ukrainian refugees, expats, students)

## 💰 Revenue Model (Dual Strategy)

### Model 1: Direct B2C
- **Pricing:** €9.99 (Basic) / €19.99 (Premium) / €149 (Lifetime)
- **Features:** Unlimited lessons, voice practice, exam prep (ÖSD/Goethe)
- **Year 1 Target:** €36,000 ARR (200 paying users)

### Model 2: Partner Distribution ⭐ **(KEY GROWTH DRIVER)**
- **Partners:** Telegram channel owners (Russian/Austrian communities)
- **Commission:** 30-40% (Bronze/Silver/Gold tiers)
- **Our Share:** 65% after partner commission
- **Year 1 Target:** €58,500 ARR from partners (61% of total revenue)

**Combined Year 1:** €94,500 ARR (3.5x better than B2C alone!)

## 🚀 Go-to-Market

### Phase 1: Launch (Months 1-3)
- Beta with 50 users → testimonials
- Launch in Telegram Bot Store
- Content marketing (YouTube, TikTok, Reddit)
- **Target:** 3 partners, 100 users

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-6)
- Telegram Ads (target RU language in Austria)
- Referral program (€5 credit per referral)
- Free webinars for lead gen
- **Target:** 6 partners, 1,000 users

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 7-12)
- White-label bot for partners
- B2B packages for language schools
- DACH expansion (Germany, Switzerland)
- **Target:** 10 partners, 5,000 users

## ⚠️ Risks

1. **ChatGPT launches German bot** → Focus on niche (Russian speakers, local content)
2. **High churn** → Gamification, community features, accountability
3. **Partner churn** → Long-term contracts, performance bonuses
4. **API costs** → Caching, usage limits, cheaper models for basic tasks
