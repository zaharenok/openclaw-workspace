# LinkedIn Content Strategy

## Hook Formulas

1. **Contrarian Hook**: "Most people think X, but actually Y..."
2. **Story Hook**: "I used to struggle with X until I discovered..."
3. **Data Hook**: "After analyzing 1000+ [things], here's what I found..."
4. **Mistake Hook**: "The #1 mistake people make with [topic]..."
5. **Secret Hook**: "Here's what nobody tells you about [topic]..."

## Post Structure

1. **Hook** (1-2 lines): Grab attention
2. **Body** (3-5 lines): Deliver value
3. **CTA** (1 line): Ask for engagement
4. **Hashtags** (3-5): Relevant tags

## Best Posting Times (Austria/Europe)

- **Tuesday-Thursday**: 9:00-11:00 AM
- **Avoid**: Weekends, early mornings, late evenings

## Hashtag Strategy

- Mix: 2-3 broad (#Tech, #AI) + 2-3 niche (#AIStrategy, #Automation)
- Place hashtags at the bottom of the post
- Create 1-2 branded hashtags for your content series

## 4-1-1 Rule

For every social media action:
- **4**: Repost/Share others' content (comments on others' posts)
- **1**: Curated content (sharing articles with your take)
- **1**: Original content (your own posts)
